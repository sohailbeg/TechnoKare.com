About Us


Welcome to TechnoKare Information Technology Pvt Ltd, your premier destination for SAP ABAP training. We believe that training is not just about sharing what we know; it's about mentoring with sheer passion and excellence. Unlike most training institutes that rely on recorded videos from external trainers, we pride ourselves on providing personalized and interactive training sessions conducted by our in-house experts. This ensures you receive continuous support and guidance throughout your learning journey and beyond.
Join us at TechnoKare Information Technology Pvt Ltd and experience the difference that dedicated, in-house training can make. Together, we will help you achieve excellence.

Modes of Traning

Live Mode

Go for instructor lead live training, connect over the online meeting tool. Respected students attend well structured Live Interactive sessions by qualified instructors and ask queries in interactive session and later review the class by videos.

Video Mode

Students learn from pre-recorded videos of the Live sessions. Learn at your own pace from anywhere at any time using any device. These self-paced videos are recorded by certified instructors. Ask your doubts via email from trainer.

Running session

Join the Ongoing batch from middle, watch the missed classes by videos and join trainer LIVE on regular basis for upcoming classes. You will also get a chance to attend next LIVE batch from beginning again.
